JUDGMENT

1. Md. Akbar Ali (hereinafter, referred to as “accused”) are before this
court facing accusation for the commission of offence U/s.363/376(AB) IPC

R/W Sec. 6 of the POCSO Act.

2. The prosecution in case brief is that the informant Nurul Islam lodged an FIR
before the IC Bazar Nagar under Dhubri PS on 06.04.18 and /nter-a/a stated that
on that day at about 11.30 am while his 3(Three) years old daughter was playing
infront of his house. The accused person induced his daughter with a Ku/ffi Malai

and took her to his house and had sexual intercourse with her. The informant
2

stated thereafter his daughter told his wife about the incident. The informant
prayed to take action against the accused person.
3. Based on the FIR the IC Bazar Town Out Post gave GD Entry No. 125 dated
06.04.18 and forwarded the case to the OC Dhubri PS for registration of a case.
Accordingly, Dhubri PS Case No. 412/18 under Section 363 IPC R/W Section 6 of
the POCSO Act. Thereafter, Section 376(AB) was added. The OC entrusted
entrusted SI Danesh Ali to investigate the case. During the course of investigation,
the accused person was arrested and was remanded to judicial custody. The police
after investigation submitted charge-sheet against the accused person under
Section 363/376(AB) and Section 6 of the POCSO Act. The learned Special Judge
transferred this case to this court for trial.
Accordingly, charge under Section 8 of the POCSO Act was framed against the
accused person. Charges were read over and explained to the accused person to
which he pleaded not guilty and claimed trial.
4. The prosecution in order to establish the guilt of the accused person examined
as many as 3 (Three) witnesses including the victim. The defence chooses not to
lead any evidence. The statement of the accused person under Section 313 Cr.P.C is
recorded. The accused person pleaded his innocence.
5. Heard both sides.
6. POINTS FOR DETERMINATION:-

Whether the accused person on 06.04.18 at about 11.30 am at village Iron
Jongla Part-III sexually assaulted the minor daughter of the informant by tough her
breast/ vagina and thereby committed an offence punishable under Section 8 of the

POCSO Act.
3
7. DISCUSSION, DECISION AND REASON THEREOF:-

I have gone through the FIR, the evidence on record and also the submission of
the learned Special Judge and the learned counsel of the accused person. The
victim in her evidence stated that the accused person is her grandfather who gave
her Ku/ff Malai. PW1 and PW2 the father and the mother of the victim stated in
their evidence that on the date of the incident there was some altercation with the
accused person. The evidence on record does not discloses that the accused person
sexually assaulted the minor daughter of the informant. Accordingly, the accused
person is acquitted from charges under Section 363/376(AB) R/W Section 8 of the
POCSO Act and set at liberty, forthwith. His bail bond shall remain in force for a
period of six months in terms of Section 437A Cr.P.C.
