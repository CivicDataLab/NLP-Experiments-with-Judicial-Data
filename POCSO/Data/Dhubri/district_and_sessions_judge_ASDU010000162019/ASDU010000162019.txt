JUDGMENT

1. Sri Bablu Bin @ Niranjan Bin (hereinafter referred to as “accused”) is
before this court facing accusation for the commission of offence U/s.

376(3)/511 R/W Sec. 8 of POCSO Act.

2. The prosecution case, in brief, is that the informant, Smt. Urmila Devi
lodged an FIR before the OC Dhubri PS on 27.09.18 and /nter-a/a stated

that on 26.01.18 at about 11.30 pm when her granddaughter, Miss 'X' was
2

sleeping at the veranda of the house. At that time, the accused person
entered in to her house and caught her granddaughter, pulled her breast
and touch her private parts with an attempt to commit raped upon her. The
informant stated that when her granddaughter shouted, the other family
members came and rescued her granddaughter, but the accused person fled
away. The informant prayed to take action against the accused person.

3. Based on the FIR, the police registered Dhubri PS Case No. 1461/18
under Section 457/354/376/511 IPC read with Section 8 of the POCSO Act.
The OC entrusted WSI Gita Sarkar to investigate the case. During the course
of the investigation, the accused person was arrested and remanded to
judicial custody. The IO after investigation of the case submitted charge-
sheet against the accused person under Section 457/354/376/511 read with
Section 8 of the POCSO Act. The learned Special Judge transferred this case
to this court for trial. Accordingly, copies were furnished to the accused
person. The charges under Section 376(3)/511 IPC and Section 8 of the
POCSO Act were framed against the accused person. The charges were read
over and explained to the accused person to which he pleaded not guilty
and claimed trial.

4. The prosecution in order to establish the guilt of the accused person
examined as many as 5 (Five) witnesses including the victim. The defence
chooses not to lead any evidence. The statement of the accused person
under Section 313 Cr.P.C is recorded. The accused person pleaded his

innocence.
5. Heard both sides.

6. POINTS FOR DETERMINATION:-

i) Whether on 26.09.18 at about 11.30 pm at Binapatti, the accused person
attempted to commit rape of the minor granddaughter of the informant and
thereby committed an offence punishable under Section 376(3)/511 IPC.

ii) Whether the accused person on same date,time and place sexually
assaulted the granddaughter of the informant and thereby committed an

offence punishable under Section 8 of the POCSO Act,2012.

7. DISCUSSION, DECISION AND REASON THEREOF:-

I have gone through the evidence on record and consider the submissions
of the learned Special Public Prosecutor and the learned counsel of the
accused person. PW1 the informant in her evidence stated that the accused
person is his grandson by relation. On the date of the incident, there was
quarrel between the accused person and her granddaughter. Due to some
mis-understanding she lodged the FIR. PW2, the victim also in her evidence
stated that on the date of the incident there was some quarrel with the
accused person who is her cousin. The other witnesses also deposed in
similar fashion.

The evidence on record does not establishes the ingredients of Section
4
376(3)/511 or of Section 8 of the POCSO Act.
Accordingly, the accused person is aquitted from the charges under
Section 376(3)/511 R/W Sec. 8 of POCSO Act and set at liberty, forthwith.
The bail bond of the accused person shall remain in force for a period of
six months in terms of Section 437A Cr.P.C.
